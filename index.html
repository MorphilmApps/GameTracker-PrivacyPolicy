Privacy Policy for Game Tracker

Effective Date: October 1st, 2025

Last Updated: October 14th, 2025

TL;DR - Privacy Summary

Your game data never leaves your device. We don't run servers, create accounts, or sell data.

Free version with ads: Google AdMob (not us) collects device identifiers, usage data, and approximate location for ad delivery. This data is not linked to your personal identity. You control tracking through iOS settings.

After purchasing "Remove Ads": No more data collection from advertising networks. Your app becomes completely private - all game data stays on your device and your optional iCloud backup.

Introduction

Morphilm Apps ("we," "our," or "us") operates the GameTracker mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and shared when you use our App.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. Game Tracker is designed to keep your game data private and secure on your device unless you explicitly give permission.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide Directly

Game Tracker stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Game Statistics: Dice rolls, scores, player names, game sessions, and related game data
  • App Settings: Your preferences for theme, notifications, and app behavior
  • Custom Content: Player profiles, game templates, and tags you create
  • Tutorial Progress: Whether you've completed the app tutorial and seen onboarding tips
Important: This data is stored exclusively on your device and in your personal iCloud account (if you enable iCloud backup). We do not have access to this information.

Information Collected Automatically (Only in Free Version with Ads)

Important: The data described below is collected by Google AdMob (not by us, Morphilm Apps) and only when ads are displayed. Once you purchase "Remove Ads," this data collection stops completely.

Google AdMob (Third-Party Advertising Service)

To support our small family business and keep the app free, we use Google AdMob to display advertisements in the free version. Google (not Morphilm Apps) may collect and process the following information:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers (such as Advertising ID/IDFA)
  • Usage Data: How you interact with ads, ad impressions, clicks
  • Location Data: Approximate location based on IP address (not precise GPS location)
  • App Activity: Which features you use and when

This information is used to:

  • Deliver relevant advertisements
  • Measure ad performance
  • Detect and prevent fraud
  • Improve ad services

Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Google AdMob Partners: https://support.google.com/admob/answer/9012903

Information We May Collect Through Feedback

When you voluntarily submit feedback through our in-app feedback form, we may collect:

  • Contact Information: Your email address (only if you choose to provide it)
  • Feedback Content: The subject and message you submit
  • Device Information: iOS version, device model, and app version (automatically included to help with technical support)

Feedback submissions are processed through Formspree, a third-party form handling service. We use this information only to respond to your inquiries and improve our app. Your email address (if provided) is used solely for responding to your feedback.

Tracking and Personalized Advertising

App Tracking Transparency

Game Tracker requests your permission to track your activity across other companies' apps and websites for personalized advertising. This is done through Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework.

When you first launch the App, you will see a system prompt asking:

"Allow Game Tracker to track your activity across other companies' apps and websites?"

If you allow tracking:

  • Google AdMob can use your Advertising ID (IDFA) to show you personalized ads based on your interests
  • Ad performance may be better targeted to your preferences
  • You may see more relevant advertisements

If you decline tracking:

  • You will still see advertisements, but they will be contextual (non-personalized)
  • Your Advertising ID will not be used for cross-app tracking
  • Ad frequency and content will not be customized based on your behavior

Changing Your Tracking Preferences

You can change your tracking preference at any time:

  1. Open the Settings app on your iPhone/iPad
  2. Scroll down and tap Game Tracker
  3. Toggle Allow Tracking on or off

Alternatively, you can manage tracking for all apps:

  1. Open the Settings app
  2. Tap Privacy & Security
  3. Tap Tracking
  4. Toggle Allow Apps to Request to Track on or off

Removing Ads = Complete Privacy

Want complete privacy? Purchase "Remove Ads" for a one-time fee.

You can purchase the Ad-Free upgrade within the App to completely remove all advertisements. When you purchase this upgrade:

  • All ads are permanently removed from your experience
  • Google AdMob stops collecting data (since ads are no longer displayed)
  • Zero tracking - No device identifiers, no usage data, no location data collected
  • 100% private - All your game data stays exclusively on your device and your optional iCloud backup
  • Your purchase is managed securely through Apple's App Store
  • The upgrade is linked to your Apple ID and persists across reinstalls

After purchasing ad removal, the only data stored is:

  • Your game statistics (stored locally on your device)
  • Your app preferences (stored locally on your device)
  • Your purchase receipt (managed securely by Apple, not accessible to us)
  • Optional iCloud backup (encrypted and controlled by you)

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

All of your game data is stored locally on your device using Apple's secure storage mechanisms. This includes:

  • Game sessions and statistics
  • Player profiles
  • Custom settings and preferences

We do not have access to this data, and it is not transmitted to our servers (we don't operate any servers for this App).

No Cloud Sync: We do not sync your game data to any cloud services or external servers operated by us. The only cloud storage used is Apple's iCloud, which is optional and controlled entirely by you through your device settings.

iCloud Backup

If you enable iCloud on your device, your game data may be backed up to your personal iCloud account. This backup is:

  • Encrypted by Apple
  • Only accessible by you through your Apple ID
  • Subject to Apple's privacy policy
  • Optional (you can disable iCloud for Game Tracker in your device settings)

Data Retention

Your game data remains on your device until you:

  • Delete the App
  • Manually delete games or data within the App
  • Disable iCloud backup (which removes cloud copies)
